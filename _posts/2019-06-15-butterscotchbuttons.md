Ingrediants:
* 125g butter
* 1 tablespoon vanilla
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 1 tablespoon golden syrup
* 1 & 1/4 cups self-raising flour

Steps:
1. Beat butter, vanilla, sugar + golden syrup
2. Mix in sifted flour
3. 150o for 30 mins
